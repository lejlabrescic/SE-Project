About Us

The Bosnia's largest athletic apparel company, Sneaker Shop is best known for its footwear, apparel, and equipment. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, the company became Sneaker Shop in 1971. One of the most valuable brands among sport businesses, Sneaker Shop employs over 76,000 people worldwide.
